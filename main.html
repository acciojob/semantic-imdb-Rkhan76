Summary

Inception is a sci-fi thriller about Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who enters people's dreams to steal secrets. He is offered a chance to have his criminal history erased if he can perform an impossible task: inception— planting an idea into someone's subconscious.

Actors

Leonardo DiCaprio as Dom Cobb, Joseph Gordon-Levitt as Arthur, Ellen Page as Ariadne, Tom Hardy as Eames, and Ken Watanabe as Saito are among the stellar cast of this film.
